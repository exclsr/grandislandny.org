Grand Island, NY

Greetings,

Grand Island, NY (map) is a friendly, suburban community that resides (on an island) between Buffalo, NY and Niagara Falls, NY. It is the home of quality schools, neighborly churches, a couple of state parks, a few golf courses, a marina, a number of small businesses, and about 18,621 residents.

Whether you are a new resident, or a visitor looking for information on our town, we hope you'll find this site to be helpful.

Cheers,

The Grand Island NY.org Team
